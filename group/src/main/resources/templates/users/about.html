About Childhelp
The nations largest and oldest non profit child abuse organization

What do we stand for?


Childhelp exists to meet the physical, emotional, educational and spiritual needs of abused, neglected and at-risk children. Childhelp believes that every child has a unique contribution to make to the world, and deserves the best that we can provide. We believe unconditional love is the foundation on which all healing begins, and that is why we are dedicated to ensuring all children, families and communities live in safe, stable, supportive, and nurturing environments that are equitable, inclusive, and free of abuse and neglect. At Childhelp,“All Who Enter Here Will Find Love.”.

Why are clothing and toy drives needed?

By donating toys and gently wore clothing items, not only does it provide a second life for used items, but can significantly impact a child's life. There are thousands of children nationwide who do not have the means to afford toys and/or clothing items. By sending your donations to ChildHelp, you are bringing joy and happiness to our children in need.

What are we accepting?

ChildHelp is currently seeking out donations in the following categories:

    1. new or gently used clothing for kids and baby
    2. new or used children's toys
    3. shoes, socks, winter clothing, sports uniforms
    4. boardgames, puzzles, children's books
    5. sports gear, equipment
