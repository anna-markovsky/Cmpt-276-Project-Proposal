Making an impact with the Childhelp Speak Up Be Safe program


In FY23, the Childhelp Tennessee Child Advocacy Center in Knoxville was awarded a grant to promote child abuse prevention education in Knox County. Through the addition of a Childhelp Speak Up Be Safe trainer, tremendous success was achieved in raising awareness throughout Knox County and successfully implementing Childhelp Speak Up Be Safe into classrooms and other community settings. FY24 is looking to have an even bigger reach in Knox County through these efforts.
